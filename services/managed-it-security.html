Services · Run

Your Microsoft environment, run and secured end to end.

We monitor identity, endpoints, cloud, and backups, resolve issues before users feel them, and harden the way attackers actually get in. One accountable partner for the whole platform — and the senior engineers who built it are the ones watching it.

01 · The problem

Attackers don't break in —
they log in.

The overwhelming majority of real-world breaches don't begin with an exotic exploit. They begin with a stolen password, a convincing email, or an unpatched device — the front door left unlocked. So running an environment well and securing it are the same job, not two purchases. We focus the spend where attackers actually are: identity and MFA in Microsoft Entra ID, email and the human layer, endpoint hardening through Intune, and tested recovery for the bad day that still gets through — a posture you can stand behind in a board review, an audit, or a cyber-insurance questionnaire.

02 · What this is

One team that runs it and secures it.

Most environments are split across a vendor for endpoints, another for cloud, a security product nobody tunes, and a queue for everything else. We run, secure, and monitor your entire Microsoft environment as one platform — a senior-led engagement, not a ticket queue, with response targets written into each agreement and quarterly vCIO reviews that keep budget, security, and growth plans aligned. Available fully managed for teams with no internal IT department, or co-managed alongside your internal team — and AI-accelerated on the toil, so senior engineers spend their time on judgment calls.

05 · Incident response

What it looks like when something does break.

07 · FAQ

Common questions.

The questions leaders ask before handing over the keys — answered the way we'd answer them in a working session.

FAQ · 01

What's the difference between fully managed and co-managed IT?

Fully managed means we are your IT department — helpdesk, infrastructure, identity, security, and strategy. Co-managed means we supplement your internal team with monitoring, escalation depth, security hardening, and project capacity. Many clients start co-managed and adjust over time.

FAQ · 02

How does managed IT relate to cybersecurity?

They're one engagement. We treat security as part of running the environment — identity and MFA in Microsoft Entra ID, endpoint hardening in Intune, email and phishing defense, and tested recovery — rather than a separate product bolted on after the fact.

FAQ · 03

How long does onboarding take?

Typically two weeks: discovery and documentation in week one, tooling deployment and baseline hardening in week two. Your team keeps working without interruption throughout.

FAQ · 04

Do you offer response-time commitments?

Yes. Response targets and escalation paths are written into each agreement — including a defined commitment for business-critical issues — rather than promised verbally. Projects such as migrations and new builds are scoped separately so your monthly cost stays predictable.
