Services · Build

Take the manual steps out of the work that runs your business.

Approvals, intake, routing, and data entry — the repetitive work that quietly eats your team's days — wired into the Microsoft cloud you already own. We design, build, and run the flows, with a human kept exactly where judgment matters.

01 · The problem

Manual handoffs are where work goes to wait.

An approval sits in someone's inbox. A document lands in a personal mailbox and never gets filed. The same figures get re-keyed from one system into another. None of it is hard — it is just slow, invisible, and easy to drop, and the cost shows up as delay, rework, and the quiet risk of a step nobody owns. We close that gap with automation built on the platform you already pay for — Power Automate, Power Apps, SharePoint, and Azure. We map the workflow, shortlist the highest-payback steps, and wire the routing, extraction, and data movement directly into the systems people already use. A person stays in the loop for sign-off and exceptions; everything else runs itself — and the same senior people who scope the automation build it and operate it afterward.

05 · FAQ

Common questions.

What teams usually want to know before automating their first workflow — answered plainly.

FAQ · 01

Do we need to buy new software to automate our workflows?

Usually not. Most of what we build runs on Power Automate, Power Apps, SharePoint, and Azure — capabilities your Microsoft 365 licensing already includes. We start by mapping what you own before recommending anything new.

FAQ · 02

What kinds of processes are a good fit for automation?

Repetitive, rules-based work with clear handoffs: approval routing, notifications, document intake and filing, data entry between systems, and status reporting. We shortlist the highest-payback workflows first rather than automating everything at once.

FAQ · 03

Where does a human stay in the loop?

Exactly where judgment or sign-off is required. Automations handle the routing, extraction, and data movement; a person approves, exception-handles, or reviews before anything irreversible happens. We design the checkpoint into the flow, not around it.

FAQ · 04

How is workflow automation priced?

Assessments are fixed-fee. Builds are scoped as fixed-price projects with clear deliverables, and ongoing flow maintenance is available as a monthly service. You always see costs before work begins.
