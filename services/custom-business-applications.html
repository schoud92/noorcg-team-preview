07 · FAQ

Common questions.

What teams ask before commissioning a custom build — ownership, licensing, and what actually ships.

FAQ · 01

Do we own the code you build?

Yes. At handover you get the full source, the repository, and the documentation — code you can read, maintain, and extend with or without us. There is no proprietary platform to rent and no per-seat tax.

FAQ · 02

Do these apps run on infrastructure we already pay for?

Almost always. Most builds live inside the Microsoft 365 and Azure environment you already license — SharePoint, SPFx, Azure Functions, and Power Platform — so there is nothing new to license and nothing new for your team to learn.

FAQ · 03

What does the technology stack look like?

Microsoft-first: SharePoint and SPFx 1.22 with a modern heft build, React and TypeScript on the front end, Azure Functions and Node.js for back-end logic, and Power Platform where low-code fits. We choose the lightest stack that solves the problem durably.

FAQ · 04

Can you point to apps you have actually shipped?

Yes. For Sabra Health Care REIT we built and maintain roughly twenty integrated line-of-business apps in production since 2020 — including a wire-transfer dashboard, a CapEx-draw dashboard, an AR-aging report, Payment Approvals, and a Lease Abstract Report — alongside 240+ automated workflows in the client's own tenant.
