The place your team finds what it needs.

Modern SharePoint intranets, knowledge bases, and document management — designed, migrated, and built on SharePoint and SPFx. We turn scattered file shares and stale portals into one governed home for content, search, and self-service.

01 · The problem

Knowledge that lives everywhere lives nowhere.

The policies, the procedures, the templates, the answer to "how do I do X" — they're real, but they're spread across a network drive nobody can navigate, a dozen Teams channels, somebody's inbox, and an intranet last touched three reorgs ago. So people ask in chat, re-create work that already exists, and store the "real" copy on their own desktop. We fix the shape of it: content consolidated into one governed SharePoint home with structure, metadata, and search that actually returns the right document — then self-service surfaces on top, so the answer is one search away instead of one favor away.

02 · What it is

One governed home for content, on the Microsoft cloud.

A portals & intranets engagement covers the full arc: the migration that consolidates your file shares into a structured SharePoint document library, the information architecture — sites, libraries, metadata, retention — that keeps it findable, and the experience layer on top: a modern communication-site intranet, a searchable knowledge base, and custom SPFx web parts for the things SharePoint doesn't ship out of the box. It's all native Microsoft 365, governed and permissioned, built on the modern heft/SPFx 1.22 toolchain — no bolt-on platform to license, no data leaving your tenant.

06 · FAQ

Common questions.

What teams ask before consolidating their content — migration, custom builds, and what it costs.

FAQ · 01

Can you migrate our old file shares into SharePoint?

Yes — it's core to what we do. We consolidated a large, unstructured on-prem NAS into a governed SharePoint document library for IQHQ. We map the content first, design the library and metadata, and migrate with permissions and version history intact.

FAQ · 02

Do you build custom SharePoint web parts?

Yes. We build custom SharePoint Framework (SPFx) web parts in React and TypeScript on the modern heft build — like the events calendar we shipped for IQHQ — for interfaces SharePoint doesn't ship out of the box, deployed to your App Catalog.

FAQ · 03

Will the intranet work on mobile and stay accessible?

Yes. We build on modern SharePoint communication sites and responsive SPFx components, with keyboard navigation, ARIA roles, and reduced-motion support designed in — tested to work on phones and assistive technology, not just desktop browsers.

FAQ · 04

How is a portal or intranet project priced?

Discovery and content mapping are fixed-fee. Migration and build are scoped as fixed-price projects with clear deliverables, and ongoing governance and support are available as a monthly managed service. You always see costs before work begins.
