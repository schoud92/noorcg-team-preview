02 · How we work

Assess, Build, Run.

Every engagement follows the same arc, whichever capability you start with. No handoff to a junior bench halfway through — the people who scope the work stay on it.

Assess

Find the highest-payback work. We map your environment, workflows, and security posture end to end, then turn what we find into a prioritized roadmap — not a slide deck. You see where the value is and what to fix first, with fixed-fee scope before any build begins.

Ship it, hardened and integrated. A working pilot in weeks with real users and real data, then production hardening: role-based access, audit trails, and integration into the Microsoft 365, SharePoint, and Azure systems you already run. Deliverables are fixed-price and clearly defined.
