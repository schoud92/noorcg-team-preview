02 · What we build

Applied AI, end to end.

The full breadth of applied AI, from Copilot to data-aware assistants to AI-run process automation — scoped to your environment, grounded in your own data, with full ownership and governance.

Copilot

Microsoft Copilot, deployed & governed

Licensing and readiness, the permissions and data-access cleanup that decides whether Copilot is safe to turn on, then governance, adoption, and reporting. We also extend it — Copilot agents and connectors that answer from your SharePoint, line-of-business systems, and policies, not just the open web.

Assistants

Data-aware AI assistants

Beyond chat: assistants that read the actual data behind your reports rather than guessing — grounded answers with citations and role-based access, built on Claude via the Anthropic API and deployed inside your security boundary.

Generative

Generative AI & content creation

AI that drafts, summarizes, and creates: first-draft reports, proposals, and correspondence; summaries of long documents and meetings; knowledge-base and marketing copy; image and visual generation. Tuned to your tone and templates, grounded in your own facts, with a person in the loop before anything ships.

Extraction

Document & image extraction

One of our deepest strengths: turning unstructured paperwork and images into clean, structured data — contracts, statements, forms, scans, and photos read into fields your systems can use. Humans stay in the loop.

Automation

AI-driven process automation

AI doing the work end to end, not just advising on it. We wire AI and Power Automate into your Microsoft 365, SharePoint, and line-of-business systems so whole processes run themselves — intake, classification, data entry, drafting, routing, and approvals — with a human checkpoint exactly where it matters.

Governance

AI for security & governance

AI introduces new risk, and helps manage it. We govern what AI can see and do: data-access reviews before Copilot goes live, role-based access, audit trails, DLP and sensitivity labeling, and zero training-data leakage. Your data stays yours, grounded at query time inside your security boundary.

05 · FAQ

Common questions.

The questions every leadership team asks before putting AI on real data — answered without the hype.

FAQ · 01

How long does it take to deploy an AI solution?

Most engagements produce a working pilot typically within a few weeks. Production hardening, governance, and rollout often follow over the weeks after, depending on integration depth.

FAQ · 02

Is our data used to train AI models?

No. We architect solutions so your data is never used for model training. Assistants are grounded in your content at query time, inside your security boundary, with full audit trails.

FAQ · 03

Which AI platforms do you work with?

We focus on the stack we run in production: Claude via the Anthropic API, Microsoft Copilot, and Azure OpenAI. We recommend based on your security, cost, and capability requirements.

FAQ · 04

How is applied AI priced?

Assessments are fixed-fee. Builds are typically scoped as fixed-price projects with clear deliverables, and ongoing optimization is available as a monthly service. You always see costs before work begins.
