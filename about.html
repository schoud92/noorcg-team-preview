About Noor

We named ourselves after light for a reason.

Noor (نور) is the Arabic word for light. It is also our standard: a senior-led, AI-accelerated, Microsoft-focused consulting boutique where every engagement should leave you with more clarity than you started with.

01 · Our story

It started by becoming
someone’s in-house tech team.

In March 2020 — the week the pandemic upended everyone’s operations — a senior Microsoft practitioner took over IT for Sabra Health Care REIT, a publicly traded real estate investment trust.

March 2020 · A deadline nobody chose

The cutover had to be clean, more secure than what it replaced, and on time.

It worked. And rather than hand the keys back, we stayed — becoming Sabra’s de-facto in-house technology team. Over the years since, that single relationship grew into roughly 20 applications and 240+ automated workflows across the Microsoft Cloud, with Azure AI reading documents that used to be processed by hand.

Noor grew out of that work: a deliberately small, senior-led boutique that builds the systems a business runs on — and then runs them. Today we do the same across managed IT, the Microsoft Cloud, custom application development, and applied AI, for clients in real estate, life sciences, and senior care.

And we hold ourselves to a belief the firms we set out to replace don’t: clarity is a deliverable, not a side effect. Every system we build comes with plain-English documentation, predictable scopes, and explanations a non-technical leader can actually follow — so the people who depend on the work are never in the dark about it.

The person who scopes your work is the person who builds it — and the person who is still accountable for it years after go-live.
